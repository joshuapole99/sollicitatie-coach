FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is it really free? +
Yes. The first 3 analyses are completely free — including the cover letter. No credit card required. After that you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited usage.
Do I need an account? +
No. You can use the tool immediately without signing up. Just paste your CV and job description and click analyse.
How accurate is the analysis? +
The AI uses Claude by Anthropic — one of the most advanced language models available. It reads both your CV and the job description and gives a detailed, relevant analysis. Results may vary depending on how detailed your CV is.
Can I use it for any job? +
Yes. The tool works for any job in any industry. The more detailed your CV and job description, the better the results.
Is my CV safe? +
Your CV is only used for the analysis and is never stored. Every analysis is a separate, anonymous API call. We do not save any personal data.
What language does it work in? +
The tool works in Dutch and English. The cover letter will be written in the same language as your CV and job description.
